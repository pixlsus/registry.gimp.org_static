I think it would be good to

I think it would be good to provide a list of search links to typical spam keyword. Some of the most prominent I remember:

http://registry.gimp.org/search/node/loft (as in loft rental)
http://registry.gimp.org/search/node/handbag (as in "$brand name handbags for less")
http://registry.gimp.org/search/node/wedding (as in wedding gowns)
http://registry.gimp.org/search/node/loan (loans of any sort)
http://registry.gimp.org/search/node/SEO (popular SEO linking)
http://registry.gimp.org/search/node/sunglasses (fake handbags $somebrand)
http://registry.gimp.org/search/node/essay (scammers take money to write your essay^w^w^wfor nothing)
http://registry.gimp.org/search/node/jersey
http://registry.gimp.org/search/node/jordan

There have been others, but not that many different ones - the majority of spammers seem to be rather unimaginative and/or fixed to the more lucrative businesses. The idea is that anyone with the required privileges can use these links to check for new spam without too much effort.
